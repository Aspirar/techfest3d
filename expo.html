NITD
Expo

We are proud to announce the NITDeXpo, TerraTechnica’s major technology and prototype exhibition. TerraTechnica is set to be bigger than ever. This document will act as a guide for you to participate, excel with and and showcase your prototype on a National Stage.

Format

ROUND 1

  • Candidates wishing to exhibit their prototype from any engineering stream on the day of fest need to register for the event on the TerraTechnica’17 website – www.terratechinca.in in teams of 1-4. (registrations to open soon!)
  • Along with it, the participants are required to fill a form describing the prototype and mail a PDF document with some images and functionalities of the prototype on 141100025@nitdelhi.ac.in with the subject of the mail as NITDexpo - <Team name> along with contact number of the team leader.

ROUND 2

  • The submissions will be then scrutinised and a list of 25 candidates will be shortlisted. Shortlisted candidates will be intimated of selection and then they can then proceed to pay the registration fees (INR 500) on TerraTechnica’17 website.
  • Registration fees is necessary since the team will be allocated separate space and stalls for exhibits.
  • Teams have to bring the prototypes for display and assessment on NITD Campus on the day of fest. The event is scheduled to be on 23rd March 2017. Changes in the schedules if any will be contacted to the team personally.
  • The prototypes will be assessed by our judges. Decision of the judges in this regard will be final and binding.
  • Criteria for assessment –
    1. Relevance
    2. Innovation
    3. Performance
    4. Design

Prizes

  • Prizes worth INR 30,000 to be won
  • Exceptional prototypes can be considered for further funding under Start-up Centre of Innovation and Entrepreneurship (SCIE), NIT Delhi – www.startupnitdelhi.ac.in
  • Opportunity to showcase your prototypes to dignitaries of the fest as well as B2B interaction with industry members.

Terra Technica '17 House Cup

The streams of NIT Delhi compete throughout the fest by ensuring participation in various events. Winners contribute with points for the houses of respective streams. The battle in on for the most dominant house at NITD. The format is as follows each stream (B.Tech CSE, ECE, ECE, MTech) forms a house.(names are requested for the same) Whenever any member of a house wins any event, points are given to the house.

Winner - 50 points. Runner up - 30 points Second Runner up - 10 points (teams events)
Winner - 25 points. Runner up - 15 points Second Runner up - 05 points (individual events)
The house with most number of points win the Trophy!!
