Initiatives

Udaan

The national report revealed that 52% of class 5 students cannot read a class 2 level text, and that around 75% of class 5 children are unable to do simple division. Udaan has come up as an urgent and a concrete step to ameliorate the present scenario. Every volunteer at Udaan has set the goal of eliminating the problem of education inequity so that every child gets an opportunity to tread on the path of progress. Because as a society, we must realize that children are the future of our country and education is the key to success.
