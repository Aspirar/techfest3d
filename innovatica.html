Innovatica

"Innovation is seeing what everybody has seen and thinking what nobody has thought"

- Dr. Albert Szent Gyorgyi

Terra Technica 2k17, the annual Tech Fest of NIT Delhi, in association with Tech Club, NIT Delhi presents INNOVATICA, an online event where each person has to present a unique innovative idea and how that idea will make our lives better.

Rules

  • All the entries should be sent to innovatica.nitd@gmail.com
  • The entries should be sent in the following format
    "Idea: " shall contain the proposed concept of the idea.
    "Functionalities: " shall contain all the functions required for the idea.
    "Importance: " shall contain the use of that idea in real life.
    Name of the file attached should be "Name of the participant_College"
    Example: Rahul_NITDelhi
  • Multiple entries are not allowed.
  • The idea should be unique. Copied idea will be disqualified.

Note: Assume that you have unlimited resources.

Last date to send your entries: 22nd March, 2017
