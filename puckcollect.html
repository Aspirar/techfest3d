Problem Statement

Two Remote Controlled robots compete. 10 pucks of each assigned colour are distributed randomly on the Arena. The aim is to collect all pucks of the assigned colour and carry them to the own home base.

Rules

  • Max Size of robot can be 30 x 30 CM.
  •   Participants can use any wireless technology for remote control.
  • Robot must not physically separate into pieces.
  • Participants are allowed to buy robotic gripper or build their own(preferred).
